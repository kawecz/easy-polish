Learning Polish from the Streets

Easy Polish is an online video series that provides Polish learners around the world with authentic learning material. We show the Polish language as it is spoken on the street and among friends at home. Our videos are regularly published on our channel on Thursdays at 7:00 p.m. and contain interviews with people from different cities in Poland, covering a wide range of topics and fluency levels. Our videos are subtitled in both English and Polish, allowing learners to follow along even as native speakers talk at their regular pace.

Street Interviews

Our main format are street interviews in which we speak with real people in the streets of Polish cities. We talk about real issues, at real speed. Simultaneous Polish and English subtitles will help you follow every word.

Super Easy Polish

For everyone who just begins their journey with the Polish language we also have videos called Super Easy Polish. In this format we show simple vocabulary and basics of grammar at slow pace suitable for beginners.

Let’s be friends.
